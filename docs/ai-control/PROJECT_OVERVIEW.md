# Project Overview

Hidur HaSTaM / CURSOR-BOT is a Hebrew-first, RTL CRM and ERP system for a STaM business: Sifrei Torah, Tefillin, Mezuzot, Megillot, scribes, inventory, sales, mediation, production tracking, and communications.

The system is intended to centralize the business workflow in one operational application:

- CRM contacts for customers, suppliers, scribes, brokers, buyers, and related parties.
- Inventory for STaM products, Torah listings, pricing, media, quantities, archive/sold state, and barcode lookup.
- Investments and capital tracking for project/business financing.
- Sales and mediation workflows, including commission and receipt tracking.
- Torah projects, sheet batches, QA bags, workflow stages, pricing, payments, and production progress.
- WhatsApp and email communication logs, campaign/broadcast support, contact activity, and future replay.
- Dashboards for ERP/CRM status, Torah finance, exceptions, liquidity, inventory valuation, and capital risk.
- Barcode and NIIMBOT printing for operational labels from phone and PC.
- Later roadmap: tasks, reminders, calendar sync, voice inbox/IVR, call intelligence, and a safe AI action assistant.

Production should remain boring and controlled: GitHub `main` is the source of truth, Vercel `cursor-bot` deploys production, and database changes require explicit approval.
